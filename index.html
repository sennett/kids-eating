About

Kids are welcome anywhere in Barcelona. Almost everywhere people will bend over backwards to accomodate the little bastards. It's lovely and nice and eveyone smiles, but it's not what we're doing here.

You know the drill. You're walking around town trying to "enjoy a day out with the family", and Little Johnny starts throwing a hissy-fit about some bullshit. You couldn't stay in the house, and now it seems that out of the house isn't good enough either. What's a parent to do when all they want is a stiff drink and a moment to forget?

That's where this website comes in. It's a list of places where it is not merely accpetable to bring kids, but actually easy. In this list you'll find:

  • Bars with play areas
  • Play areas next to bars
  • Restaurants with colouring-in stations
  • Restaurants with child-care (seriously)
  • Highchairs
  • Changing stations
  • Anything that stops you destroying their minds with creepy Youtube videos

In short, anywhere you can easily get a drink whilst not holding a child will feature. In this list, kids fit around adults, but they also leave you the hell alone.

Key

Here are what the emojis mean:

💺
Highchairs. The more 💺, the more highchairs are generally available.
🎲
Denotes a playarea close to adult seating. More 🎲 means increasing ease in this area, up to and including dedicated child-care.
☕
Reasonable coffee. Most places have coffee.
🥐
Good breakfast-type things, pastries etc.
🍷
Alcohol. The more the merrier.
🍽️
Food. More is better if you want a proper meal.
💩
Ease of changing. 💩 = bench or wall or similar outside, close by. 💩💩 = dedicated changing station.
