Rescuing parents
in the city.
✕

Kids are welcome in cafes and restaurants in Barcelona. Almost everywhere people will bend over backwards to accommodate families.

But it's rarely easy to take kids places, especially in a pinch, when you all need a break from exploring the city.

That's where this list comes in. It's a list of places where it is not merely accpetable to bring kids, but actually easy. In this list you'll find:

  • Bars with play areas
  • Play areas next to bars
  • Restaurants with colouring-in stations
  • Restaurants with child-care (seriously)
  • Highchairs
  • Changing stations
  • Anything that stops you destroying their minds with creepy Youtube videos

In short, anywhere you can easily relax with food (or a stiff drink) whilst not holding a child will feature. In this list, kids fit around adults, but they also leave you alone. Does this resonate? Let us know!

Key

This is what the emojis mean:

💺
Highchairs. The more 💺, the more highchairs are generally available.
🎲
Denotes a playarea close to adult seating. More 🎲 means increasing ease in this area, up to and including dedicated child-care.
☕
Reasonable coffee. Most places have coffee.
🥐
Good breakfast-type things, pastries etc.
🍷
Alcohol. Most places have some form of alcohol, so they get a 🍷. Whereever it is more acceptable to drink there are two: 🍷🍷.
🍽️
Food. More is better if people need a proper meal.
💩
Ease of changing. 💩 = bench or wall or similar outside, close by. 💩💩 = dedicated changing station.

The more an emoji is repeated, the better that place is at that thing. Useful/not useful? Please let us know here!
